How you use it

Two people, one conversation

🔊

If you're hearing

Speak into the mic or type — SignBridge transcribes it instantly so the conversation is never one-sided.

⇄
🤟

If you sign

Sign to your camera — hand-landmark recognition detects each sign and speaks it aloud, no typing needed.

How it works

From gesture to voice in four steps

01

Camera opens

Grant camera access and MediaPipe tracks your hand landmarks live, entirely in your browser.

02

Sign is matched

Finger and joint positions are compared against your gesture vocabulary to identify the sign.

03

Voice replies

The matched word is spoken aloud instantly using your browser's built-in text-to-speech.

04

It's logged

Every exchange is saved to your conversation history so nothing is lost after you close the tab.
